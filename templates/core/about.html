Our Story

From a small kitchen in Thika to your special celebrations - baking dreams into reality since 2018.

The Beginning

Everest Cakes was born from a simple passion - the joy of creating something beautiful that brings happiness to others. What started as baking birthday cakes for family and friends in our small kitchen in Thika has grown into a beloved bakery serving customers across Kenya.

Our founder, inspired by the majestic Mount Kenya visible from Thika, named the bakery "Everest Cakes" - symbolizing our commitment to reaching new heights in cake artistry and customer satisfaction.

Every cake we create tells a story - your story. Whether it's a wedding, birthday, anniversary, or any special moment, we pour our heart and skill into making it memorable.

Made with Love
